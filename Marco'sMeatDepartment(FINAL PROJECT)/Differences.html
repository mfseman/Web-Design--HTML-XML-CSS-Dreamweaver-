Differences:

The Differences between The English and Chuck Roast:

Shoulder Clod (English Roast) More lean than the chuck roll. Also used to make beef stew.
Chuck Roll (Chuck Roast) More Fatty, but has more flavor. Most Ideal for pot roast.

The Difference Between Scottish Salmon and Atlantic Salmon:

Atlantic Salmon Fillets More mild than the Scottish salmon.
Scottish Salmon More fat, however has more flavorful than the Atlantic salmon.

 

The Difference between T-Bone and PorterHouse T-Bone:

T-Bone The T-Bone only has the New York Strip on one side.
Porterhouse The Porterhouse has both a fillet and new york strip steak.
