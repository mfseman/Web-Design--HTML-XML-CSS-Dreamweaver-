Types of Foods:

Ground Foods:
We have a variety of different ground foods at my meat department. Whether it would be organic, flavorful, or lean.We Take great pride of our company and make sure we give our customers the best quality meats that they deserve. All of our products are all natural meaning they do not have any steroids injected in our meats. Here are the different types of meats that we have at our market:
Ground Round The most popular ground beef at our market. Ground Round is the balance between sirloin and Chuck. The Percentage of lean to fat is 85% lean, 15% fat.
Ground Sirloin Ground Sirloin is the leanest of the ground beefs. the percentage of lean to fat ratio is 90 - 10.
Ground Chuck Ground chuck is the fattiest of them all, but the most flavourful. Ground chuck is a combination of fats from beef including the chain from the beef tenderloin grinded together with the sirloin.
Open Prairie Ground Round Organic ground beef that is fed grass, but then fed grain for a little fat. The lean to fat ratio is the same as the ground round. it is a grinded open prairie english roast.
Ground Chicken Ground chicken is a grouded whole chicken breast with the fat included in it.
Ground Turkey Ground Turkey is a grounded turkey breast without any fat on it.
Ground Pork A grinded pork loin roast.
Meatloaf Mix 1/3 Beef, 1/3 Veal, 1/3 Pork

Patties/Burgers:

Here at My Meat Department, we carry both fresh and frozen burgers. We make fresh patties beef patties, and we can also make any type of burgers at your request.

Ground Round Patties Ground round patties are 85 - 15 lean to fat ratio. Freshly made daily.
Ground Sirloin Patties Ground Sirloin patties are 90 - 10 lean to fat ratio.
Turkey Burgers Peppers, turkey, and seasonings mixed together.
Garden Chicken Burgers Feta, chicken, parsleys, and seasoning mixed togther.
Bacon Cheddar Burgers Bacon, Cheddar, and ground sirloin.
Peterluger Burgers Scallions, ground sirloin, and seasonings.
Lamb patties 30% lamb 70% beef.

Steaks:

All of our steaks are all natural meaning they do not have any antibiotics inserted in them.

New York Strip An inch thick steak that weighs roughly 4/5th of a pound.
Delmonico The same as a ribeye, just without the bone. Other names include "boneless ribeye".
Ribeye Bone-in Delmonico. Some people like the bone in because it has more flavor.
Flank We have both marinated flank steak and non marinated. The marinated is very flavorful.
Porterhouse T-Bone A porterhouse T-Bone has a fillet on one side of the bone, and a new york strip on the other.
Cubed A cubed steak is a cubed top of the sirloin tip.
