Starting Out

Basic Equipment

If you are just starting out, you probably will need these items:

  • Cutting board
  • Medium sized knife
  • Inexpensive knife sharpener
  • A refrigerator (small is OK)
  • A slow cooker (or crock pot)
  • Some crock pot recipes
