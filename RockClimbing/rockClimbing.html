Rock Climbing Fun has recently opened a school at the base of Mount Rainier. We have programs for advanced climbers as well as for beginners. Rock Climbing is a widely popular sport, especially in the state of Washington. Classes run from early morning to mid-afternoon, and private climbing lessons are available. come join our classes and experience the fun and great exercise that rock climbing can provide. Call 1-800-555-CLIMB for more information.

Equipment needed to get started:

  • Comfortable climbing shoes
  • Backpack
  • Harness

Key terms to know:

Belay
To protect a climber from falling by attaching a rope to the climber and fastening the rope to something secure
Belay On
This is called out when the belay rope is secure
